Cloud

Content and services without boundaries

From online banking and remote patient monitoring solutions to on-demand entertainment and communications platforms, the cloud has become an integral part of how businesses deliver content and services. Cloud technologies–especially combined with sensors and mobile devices–enable an increasingly larger and more widely distributed user base to access real-time solutions anytime, anywhere. As consumer and business environments become more global and their users more mobile, software systems must become more interconnected than ever. To meet user expectations and needs, cloud systems must be elastically scalable, provide fast and uninterrupted performance, and protect large volumes of confidential information.

How Synclovis helps

Synclovis collaborates with businesses across industry segments to architect, develop, and deploy secure, scalable, cloud-based products and solutions. We empower our customers to deliver cutting- edge products, from architecting and designing entertainment systems scalable to hundreds of millions of users, to developing and deploying cloud emergency medical systems that literally save lives. Synclovis understands that “cloud” is not a new name for SaaS or virtualization, but a new computing paradigm. The ability to dynamically create, destroy, and configure inexpensive compute resources “elastically” and on-demand provides new business capabilities and rewards a new approach to software architecture. Our deep mobile expertise, as well as our “machine to machine” (M2M) and Big Data analytics skills, complement our cloud capabilities. Our cloud technology competency and cross-industry expertise enables us to successfully design and develop cloud-based solutions where performance, scalability and security are critical.
